†Pachytylopsis persenairei (de Borre, 1875)
中文名：无
|
Pachytylopsis persenairei was named by de Borre (1875). It is an impression. Its type locality is Belle et Bonne colliery, Jemappes, which is in a Westphalian C terrestrial siliciclastic in the Flénu Formation of Belgium. It is the type species of Pachytylopsis.
