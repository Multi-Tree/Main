†Idelopsocus arcuatus Martynov 1928 
中文名：无
  |
  (winged insect)
  Insecta - Hypoperlida - Hypoperlidae

  PaleoDB taxon number: 229970

  Alternative combinations: Dinopsocus arcuatus, Martynopsocus arcuatus

  Full reference: A. V. Martynov. 1928. Permian fossil Insects of North-East Europe. Travaux du Musée Géologique prés l'Académie des Sciences de l'URSS 4:1-118

  Belongs to Idelopsocus according to A. P. Rasnitsyn 1996

  See also Carpenter 1992, Karny 1930 and Martynov 1928

  Sister taxa: Idelopsocus diradiatus, Idelopsocus galinae, Idelopsocus incommendatus, Idelopsocus levis, Idelopsocus mutovinus, Idelopsocus splendens, Idelopsocus tataricus

  Type specimen: PIN 2050/11(12), a forewing (right forewing). Its type locality is Soyana (PIN collection 2050), which is in a Roadian lagoonal limestone in the Iva-Gora Beds Formation of the Russian Federation.

  Ecology:

  Average measurements (in mm): forewing length 11.5

  Age range: 272.5 to 268.0 Ma

  Distribution: found only at Soyana (PIN collection 2050)
