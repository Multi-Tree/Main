Trachycladus spinispirulifer (Carter, 1879)
中文名：无
|
The orange wall sponge （Trachycladus spinispirulifer） may grow to 20 cm thick and up to 2m in length. It is a very large firm orange-skinned sponge with a yellow interior. It grows as a wall and does not show obvious oscula.

This sponge lives on rocky reefs in 5-350m of water
