Geodia neptuni (Sollas, 1886)
	中文名：无
	|
  Geodia neptuni. A large, barrel shaped sponge, squat, with very thick walls. Commonly the barrels are irregular or incomplete. It has a tough texture, and the outside is irregular and rough. Colour is brown, sometimes with reddish shades.
