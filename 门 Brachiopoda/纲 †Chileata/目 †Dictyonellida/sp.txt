Dictyonellida is a brachiopod order within the Chileata, bearing a colleplax. Eichwaldioidea is a brachiopod superfamily comprising the EIchwaldiidae and the Isogrammidae.

Eodictyonella is a genus of brachiopod. Very similar, and once proposed as synonymous with, Eichwaldia Billings 1858.

Atrypa coralifera was named by Hall (1852). It is the type species of Eodictyonella.

It was recombined as Dictyonella coralifera by Hall (1868) and Bassett and Cocks (1974); it was recombined as Eodictyonella coralifera by Rubel (2011).
