Phyllomenia is a genus of sterrofustian solenogasters, shell-less, worm-like, marine mollusks.

Phyllomenia austrina is a species of molluscs in the family Phyllomeniidae. Phyllomenia austrina form shallow marine sediments. It has sexual reproduction.
